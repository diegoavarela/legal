## 3. Medical Disclaimer

**IMPORTANT: HeartStrong is NOT a medical device and is NOT intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease or health condition.**

### 3.1 Not Medical Advice
- The App provides fitness guidance only, not medical advice
- Workout recommendations are general in nature and not individually prescribed
- RPE (Rate of Perceived Exertion) guidance is for fitness purposes only
- Always consult your transplant team or cardiologist before starting any exercise program

### 3.2 Transplant-Specific Considerations
- Heart transplant recipients have a denervated heart with unique physiological responses
- Traditional heart rate training zones do not apply to transplant recipients
- Extended warmup and cooldown periods are essential
- Your individual response to exercise may differ significantly

### 3.3 When to Stop Exercise
Stop exercising immediately and seek medical attention if you experience:
- Chest pain or pressure
- Severe shortness of breath
- Dizziness or lightheadedness
- Irregular heartbeat or palpitations
- Unusual swelling in extremities
- Nausea during exercise
- Extreme fatigue beyond normal exertion
