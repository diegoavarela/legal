# InkMark Support

Welcome to InkMark support. We're here to help you get the most out of your writing experience.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Getting Started

**How do I create a new document?**
Click the "+" button in the sidebar or use the keyboard shortcut `Cmd + N`.

**What markup formats are supported?**
InkMark supports Markdown, HTML, and LaTeX. The app automatically detects the format based on your content or you can set it manually.

**How do I see the preview?**
Use `Cmd + P` to toggle the preview panel, or click the preview icon in the toolbar.

### iCloud Sync

**Are my documents synced across devices?**
Yes, if you're signed into iCloud, your documents sync automatically across all your Mac, iPhone, and iPad devices.

**Can I use InkMark offline?**
Absolutely. Your documents are stored locally and sync when you're back online.

### Voice Transcription

**How do I use voice transcription?**
Press `Cmd + R` or click the microphone icon to start recording. Speak clearly and InkMark will transcribe your words into text.

**What languages are supported for transcription?**
Voice transcription supports the languages available in your system's Speech Recognition settings.

### Export & Sharing

**What export formats are available?**
You can export your documents as PDF, Markdown (.md), or plain text (.txt).

**How do I export a document?**
Go to File > Export or use the export button in the toolbar.
