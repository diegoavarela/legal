## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**What is Making Miles?**
Making Miles is a travel tracking app that helps you visualize countries you've visited, plan future trips, and document your travel memories.

**Is Making Miles free?**
Yes! The core features are free, including tracking visited countries, viewing the world map, and basic statistics. Premium unlocks additional features like USA states tracking, multiple passports, unlimited blog entries, and more.

**Does Making Miles work offline?**
Yes! The app works entirely offline. An internet connection is only needed for iCloud sync and web profile features.

---

### Tracking Countries

**How do I mark a country as visited?**
Go to the Countries tab and tap the "+" button, or tap directly on the world map and select "Mark as Visited".

**What's the difference between Visited and Wishlist?**
- **Visited:** Countries you've already been to
- **Wishlist:** Countries you want to visit in the future

**Can I track US states?**
Yes! With Making Miles Pro, you can track individual US states separately from country tracking.

---

### Travel Profile

**What is a Travel Profile?**
Your Travel Profile is an optional public page showing your travel statistics. You can claim a unique username and share your profile at beenthere.app/yourusername.

**How do I get a Travel Profile?**
1. Go to Settings > Public Profile
2. Sign in with Apple
3. Claim your unique username

**Is my profile public?**
You control your profile visibility in Settings. You can make it completely private or choose what to display.

### Premium Subscription

**What's included in Making Miles Pro?**
- Multiple passports support
- USA states tracking
- Unlimited blog entries
- Unlimited wishlist countries
- Advanced climate and economic data
- Detailed visa management
- iCloud sync across devices
- Data export and backup
- Share cards

**How do I subscribe?**
Tap on any locked feature or go to Settings and tap "Upgrade to Pro".

**How do I restore my purchase?**
Go to the Premium screen and tap "Restore Purchases".

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone
2. Tap your name at the top
3. Tap "Subscriptions"
4. Find Making Miles and tap "Cancel Subscription"

---

### Data & Sync

**How do I backup my data?**
Go to Settings > Export Data to create a JSON backup file.

**How do I restore from backup?**
Go to Settings > Import Data and select your backup file.

**Does my data sync across devices?**
With iCloud enabled, your data syncs automatically across all your Apple devices signed into the same Apple ID.

---

### Troubleshooting

**My countries aren't showing on the map**
Try force-closing the app and reopening it. If the issue persists, go to Settings > Reset Map Cache.

**Purchase not recognized**
Tap "Restore Purchases" in the app to restore your subscription.

**Data not syncing**
- Ensure you're signed into iCloud on your device
- Check that Making Miles has iCloud permissions in Settings
- Make sure you have an internet connection

**The app is not responding**
Try force-closing the app and reopening it. If the issue persists, restart your device.
