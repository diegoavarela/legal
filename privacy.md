# Privacy Policy

**Civics 100 - US Citizenship Test Prep**

*Last updated: January 18, 2025*

## Overview

Civics 100 ("the App") is designed to help users study for the United States Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS) naturalization test. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.**

The App operates entirely offline and does not:
- Collect personal information
- Track your location
- Access your contacts
- Store data on external servers
- Use analytics or tracking services
- Share any information with third parties

## Local Data Storage

The App stores the following data **locally on your device only**:
- Your study progress (mastered questions)
- Your selected state for personalized answers
- App preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

## Microphone and Speech Recognition

The App requests microphone access **solely** for the reading practice feature, which helps you practice pronunciation.

- All speech recognition is processed **locally on your device**
- Audio is never recorded, stored, or transmitted
- You can use the App without granting microphone permission

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App is designed as an educational tool suitable for all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
