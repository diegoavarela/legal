# ZephTicker Support

Need help with ZephTicker? We're here to assist you.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I add stocks to my watchlist?**
Use the search bar to find any stock by ticker symbol or company name, then click the + button to add it to your watchlist.

**How do I reorder my watchlist?**
Drag and drop stocks in your watchlist to reorder them.

**Data seems delayed or missing**
ZephTicker uses real-time market data. If data appears delayed, check your internet connection and try refreshing.

**How do I change the currency display?**
Go to Settings to change your preferred currency display.
