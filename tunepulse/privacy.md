# Privacy Policy for TunePulse

**Last Updated: December 23, 2024**

## Overview

TunePulse ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

## Information We Collect

### We Do NOT Collect:
- Personal information
- Audio recordings
- Location data
- Usage analytics
- Device identifiers

### Local Data Only
TunePulse processes audio input solely on your device in real-time for pitch detection. This audio data is:
- Never recorded
- Never stored
- Never transmitted to any server

### Microphone Access
The app requires microphone access exclusively for the tuner feature. Audio input is processed locally and immediately discarded after pitch detection.

### Purchase Information
If you subscribe to TunePulse Pro, payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment details.

## Data Storage

All app preferences and settings are stored locally on your device using standard iOS mechanisms. This data is not accessible to us and is not transmitted anywhere.

## Third-Party Services

TunePulse does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

TunePulse does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

**Email:** tunepulse@diegovarela.com

## Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. You can remove all local app data by uninstalling TunePulse from your device.
