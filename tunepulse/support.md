# TunePulse Support

Welcome to TunePulse support. Find answers to common questions below.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**What is TunePulse?**
TunePulse is an all-in-one music toolkit featuring a metronome, chromatic tuner, and beat machine designed for musicians of all levels.

**Is TunePulse free?**
The Metronome is completely free. The Tuner and Beat Machine require a TunePulse Pro subscription.

**Does TunePulse work offline?**
Yes! TunePulse works entirely offline. No internet connection is required.

---

### Metronome

**What tempo range is supported?**
The metronome supports tempos from 20 to 300 BPM.

**How do I use tap tempo?**
Tap the tempo display repeatedly to set the BPM based on your tapping rhythm.

---

### Tuner

**What instruments are supported?**
TunePulse supports:
- Guitar (Standard, Drop D, DADGAD, Open G, Half Step Down)
- Bass 4-String (Standard, Drop D, Drop C, Half Step Down)
- Bass 5-String (Standard, Drop A)
- Ukulele (Standard, Baritone, Low G)
- Violin (Standard)
- Chromatic mode for any instrument

**Why isn't the tuner detecting my instrument?**
- Make sure microphone permissions are enabled in Settings > TunePulse
- Try playing closer to your device's microphone
- Ensure you're in a quiet environment
- Check that you're using the correct instrument/tuning setting

**What is Chromatic mode?**
Chromatic mode detects any musical note without being locked to a specific instrument or tuning. Perfect for tuning any instrument.

---

### Beat Machine

**How do I create a beat pattern?**
Tap on the grid squares to toggle beats on/off. Each row represents a different drum sound, and each column represents a beat in the pattern.

---

### Subscription

**How do I subscribe to TunePulse Pro?**
Tap on the Tuner or Beat Machine tab, then select your preferred subscription plan (Monthly or Yearly).

**How do I restore my purchase?**
Go to Settings within the app and tap "Restore Purchases".

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone
2. Tap your name at the top
3. Tap "Subscriptions"
4. Find TunePulse and tap "Cancel Subscription"

**Subscription Pricing:**
- Monthly: $1.99/month
- Yearly: $19.99/year (save 17%)

---

### Troubleshooting

**The app is not responding**
Try force-closing the app and reopening it. If the issue persists, restart your device.

**Microphone permission issues**
Go to Settings > TunePulse and ensure Microphone access is enabled.

**Purchase not recognized**
Tap "Restore Purchases" in the app's Settings screen to restore your subscription.
